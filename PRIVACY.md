# Privacy Policy

**Nepali Calendar - Patro**
*Last updated: March 8, 2026*

---

## Overview

Nepali Calendar - Patro ("the App") is a macOS menu bar utility developed by Noble Stack. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## Data We Collect

### Anonymous Usage Analytics

The App uses **Aptabase**, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage events such as:

- App launches and popover opens
- Feature usage (e.g., which tab was opened, which menu bar style was selected)
- Error events (e.g., failed network requests for weather or prices)

This data is **fully anonymous** — no personal identifiers, device IDs, IP addresses, or Apple IDs are collected or stored. Analytics are used solely to understand which features are popular and to improve the App.

### No Personal Data

The App does **not** collect:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data (weather uses fixed Kathmandu coordinates, not your location)
- Photos, files, or documents
- Browsing history or search queries
- Financial or payment information
- Health or fitness data

---

## Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following external services to provide its features:

| Service | Purpose | Data Sent |
|---|---|---|
| **Open-Meteo** (open-meteo.com) | Kathmandu weather | Fixed GPS coordinates for Kathmandu only |
| **goldprice.org** | Gold and silver prices in NPR | Standard HTTP request (no user data) |
| **Nepal Oil Corporation** (noc.org.np) | Fuel prices | Standard HTTP request (no user data) |
| **RSS Feeds** | Nepal news headlines | Standard HTTP request (no user data) |
| **Aptabase** (aptabase.com) | Anonymous analytics | Anonymous event names only |

No personal information is shared with any of these services.

---

## Data Storage

All App data (cached weather, prices, settings, and preferences) is stored **locally on your Mac** using standard macOS UserDefaults. No data is stored on external servers owned by us.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children. The App is rated 4+ and contains no age-restricted content.

---

## Your Rights

Since the App collects no personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can disable analytics by removing the App from your Mac.
