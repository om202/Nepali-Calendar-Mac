## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I access the app?
Click the Nepali flag icon (🇳🇵) in your Mac's menu bar to open the popover. The app lives entirely in the menu bar — there is no dock icon or app window.

### How do I change what's shown in the menu bar?
Open the popover → click **Settings** → choose from 8 display styles under **Menu Bar Display**. Options include Nepali date & time, English date & time, day with date, and more.

### How often are prices updated?
Gold, silver, and fuel prices refresh automatically every 24 hours. Weather updates periodically throughout the day.

### Why are gold/silver prices showing as "stale"?
This means the cached prices are older than 24 hours and a fresh update hasn't completed yet. This can happen if you're offline or the data source is temporarily unavailable. Prices will update automatically when connectivity is restored.

### Can I browse past or future dates?
Yes. Use the **left/right arrows** (‹ ›) on the date section to navigate backward and forward. Tap **आज** to return to today.

### How does the date converter work?
Open the popover → scroll down to **Date Converter**. Toggle between **BS → AD** and **AD → BS** modes, select a date using the dropdown pickers, and the converted result appears instantly.

### Does the app collect my data?
We use privacy-respecting analytics (Aptabase) to understand basic usage patterns such as which features are popular. **No personal data, location, or browsing history is collected.** The app makes network requests only for weather, prices, and news — all data stays on your Mac.

### News articles aren't loading. What should I do?
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Close and reopen the popover — news refreshes on open.
3. If the issue persists, the RSS source may be temporarily unavailable. Try again later.

### How do I make the app start automatically?
Open the popover → click **Settings** → toggle **Launch at Login** on.

### How do I quit the app?
Click the **✕** button in the top-right corner of the popover, or use the keyboard shortcut **⌘Q** while the popover is open.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| App not visible in menu bar | Check **System Settings → Control Center → Menu Bar Only** — ensure items aren't hidden |
| Prices not updating | Verify internet connection; prices refresh every 24 hours |
| Wrong time displayed | The app shows Nepal Time (UTC+5:45), not your local time — this is by design |
| News feed empty | Check internet connectivity; try closing and reopening the popover |

## Privacy Policy

Nepali Calendar - Patro does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. The app uses:

- **Open-Meteo API** for weather data (no API key, no tracking)
- **goldprice.org** for metal prices
- **noc.org.np** for fuel prices
- **RSS feeds** for news headlines
- **Aptabase** for anonymous, privacy-first usage analytics

All data is cached locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences. No data is sent to any server owned by us.
